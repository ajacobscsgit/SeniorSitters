Transportation & Outing Details

To keep pricing simple and transparent, SeniorSitters uses flat travel fees based on distance for all outings.

Travel Fee (per visit)

Local (0–10 miles): $20
Standard (10–20 miles): $30
Extended (20+ miles): $45+ (quoted in advance)

What's Included

  • Transportation coordination
  • Time accompanying the client
  • Wait time during appointments
  • Return trip support

Important Notes

  • All outings must be pre-approved
  • Travel fees apply only to client-related trips
  • Personal or additional stops are not covered
  • Mileage is internally tracked; clients are charged flat rates for simplicity

Service Scope

SeniorSitters provides non-medical companionship and daily support designed to enhance quality of life.

  • No medical or nursing services
  • No medication administration
  • No emergency medical care
  • No heavy personal care (bathing, lifting, toileting)

For families needing additional care, we're happy to help guide you to appropriate resources.
