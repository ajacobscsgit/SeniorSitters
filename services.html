Our Services

We provide warm, non-medical companion care designed to enhance quality of life while respecting seniors' independence and dignity.

💬

Companion Visits

Regular friendly visits providing meaningful social interaction, conversation, and companionship. We engage in activities like playing games, reading together, reminiscing, or simply sharing a cup of tea. Our visits are designed to combat loneliness and provide the social connection that is so important for well-being.

🍽️

Meal Reminders & Light Meal Support

Gentle reminders for meals and hydration, with light assistance in preparing simple meals or snacks. We can also help with meal planning and ensure seniors have nutritious food available. Our sitters can prepare light meals, warm up leftovers, or assist with meal setup.

🛒

Errands & Outings

Assistance with grocery shopping, picking up prescriptions, banking, and other routine errands. We can also accompany seniors on outings to appointments, social events, religious services, or community activities. Safe transportation and accompaniment is a key part of our service.

🏥

Appointment Companionship

Accompanying seniors to medical appointments, dental visits, or other healthcare appointments. We can help by taking notes, providing transportation, and ensuring the senior feels comfortable and supported during their visit. We communicate with family members about appointment outcomes.

🏠

Light Household Help

Assistance with light housekeeping tasks such as organizing, light cleaning, laundry assistance, and maintaining a safe living environment. We help with tasks that support independence while ensuring the home remains safe and comfortable.

💆

Respite Support for Family Caregivers

Providing breaks for family caregivers so they can rest, run errands, or take time for themselves. Whether you need a few hours or a regular schedule, we step in to provide quality companionship while you take care of your own well-being.

📞

Wellness Check-Ins

Regular phone or in-person check-ins to ensure seniors are doing well. We verify their well-being, check that they have what they need, and provide friendly conversation. These check-ins give families peace of mind when they cannot be there in person.

What We Don't Provide

It's important to understand the scope of our non-medical companion care services.

Medical Services

We do not provide nursing, medical treatment, wound care, or any form of clinical medical assistance.

Medication Administration

Our sitters do not administer medications, manage prescriptions, or provide medical monitoring.

Emergency Medical Services

We are not a medical emergency service. For emergencies, please call 911 immediately.

Heavy Personal Care

We do not provide assistance with bathing, toileting, or other intimate personal care tasks.
