Understanding Eligibility

Here's a guide to help you understand if SeniorSitters is the right fit.

✓ Likely Eligible

  • Needs companionship and social interaction
  • Needs help with errands and outings
  • Needs meal reminders or light meal support
  • Needs light non-medical household help
  • Family wants regular check-ins for peace of mind
  • Needs respite from family caregivers

⚠ May Need Referral Instead

  • Needs nursing or skilled medical care
  • Needs medication administration
  • Needs wound care or medical treatments
  • Needs emergency supervision 24/7
  • Needs medical transport with clinical support
  • Heavy personal care (bathing, toileting assistance)
