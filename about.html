About SeniorSitters

SeniorSitters was built with a simple mission: to provide seniors with the companionship they deserve and give families the peace of mind they need.

We believe that every senior deserves to feel valued, connected, and supported. Our non-medical companion care services are designed to enhance quality of life while respecting each individual's dignity and independence.

Whether it's a friendly visit, assistance with errands, or simply someone to talk to, our sitters are dedicated to making a positive difference in the lives of seniors and their families.

What Makes SeniorSitters Different

We're not a medical facility or a large corporation. We're a local, family-focused service built on personal connections.

Local & Personal

We're proud to serve the Greater Cleveland community. We know the area, understand local needs, and build real relationships with families we serve.

Non-Medical Focus

Our services are clearly defined as non-medical companion care. We provide friendship and light assistance — not nursing or medical treatment.

Flexible & Customized

Every family has different needs. We work with you to create a care plan that fits your schedule and preferences.

Consistent Sitters

We strive to match the same sitter with each senior whenever possible, building trust and familiarity over time.

Family Communication

We keep families informed with regular updates about their loved one's well-being and any notable observations.

Transparent Pricing

No hidden fees or surprises. We discuss pricing upfront and work with families to find care that fits their budget.

Important Information

SeniorSitters provides non-medical companion care only. We do not provide nursing, medical treatment, medication administration, or emergency medical services.

Our sitters are trained to provide companionship, light assistance with daily activities, and friendly support. They are not medical professionals and cannot provide any form of medical care.

For any medical emergencies, please call 911 immediately. If a senior in our care experiences a medical situation, our sitters will contact emergency services and family members as appropriate.
