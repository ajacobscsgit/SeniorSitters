Join the SeniorSitters Team

Make a meaningful impact while building a flexible and rewarding career.

Help seniors stay active, safe, and connected with compassionate companionship and gentle daily support.

Responsibilities

  • Friendly conversation and companionship
  • Assisting with daily routines (non-medical)
  • Light housekeeping like tidying and dishes
  • Meal preparation and reminders
  • Accompanying clients on outings or appointments
  • Providing updates to families after visits

Pay & Compensation

SeniorSitters provides dependable weekly pay and meaningful compensation for the time you spend supporting clients.

  • Starting pay: $17/hour
  • Weekly pay that is consistent and reliable
  • Mileage reimbursement for approved client outings
  • Paid time for outings and appointments

Travel outside of approved client-related trips is not reimbursed.

Schedule & Flexibility

  • Flexible scheduling based on availability
  • Part-time and consistent weekly opportunities
  • Choose preferred days and times

Benefits & Perks

  • Weekly pay
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Paid outings to restaurants, parks, and events
  • Mileage reimbursement for approved travel
  • Supportive and respectful work environment
  • Opportunity for growth within the company
  • Performance-based bonuses available
  • Company badge/ID provided
  • Branded t-shirts for outings and events

Holidays & Time Off

  • Recognition of major holidays
  • Eid Al-Fitr and Eid Al-Adha observed with 2 days off
  • Holiday shifts may include increased pay

Requirements

  • Reliable transportation
  • Valid driver’s license
  • Strong communication skills
  • Compassionate and dependable personality
  • Ability to pass a background check
  • Must be punctual and professional
