Become a SeniorSitter

Join our team of compassionate caregivers and make a meaningful difference in the lives of seniors and their families.

What Our Sitters Do

As a SeniorSitter, you'll provide friendly companionship and light non-medical assistance to seniors in your community.

💬 Companionship

Engage in friendly conversation, play games, read together, or simply provide a comforting presence.

🛒 Errand Support

Assist with grocery shopping, picking up prescriptions, and other routine errands.

🍽️ Meal Support

Help with meal preparation, provide meal reminders, or accompany seniors to meals.

🏥 Appointment Accompaniment

Accompany seniors to medical appointments and provide transportation.

🏠 Light Household Help

Assist with light housekeeping tasks like organizing, laundry help, and tidying up.

📞 Wellness Check-Ins

Perform regular check-ins to ensure seniors are doing well and have what they need.

Requirements & Ideal Qualities

📋 Requirements

• Must be 18 years or older
• Pass background check
• Have reliable transportation
• Available for flexible scheduling
• Good communication skills
• Responsible and reliable

💝 Ideal Qualities

• Genuine compassion for seniors
• Patient and understanding
• Reliable and dependable
• Good listener
• Respectful of dignity and privacy
• Warm and friendly personality

🌟 What We Offer

• Flexible hours that fit your schedule
• Competitive compensation
• Training and support
• Make a real difference
• Build meaningful relationships
• Grow in your caregiving career
